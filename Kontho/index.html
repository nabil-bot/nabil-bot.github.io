Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Nms Kontho deals with user data.


User-Generated Data:

You can save and manage words using this app. Your dictionary file (.pkl) is stored on your device, and we don't access or share it with any third parties.


Clipboard History:

If you opt to enable the "Track Clipboard" feature, your clipboard history is stored locally on your device. You retain full control to delete or modify this information as needed. You have the option to contribute to our dictionary by adding a word to our Google Firebase data server. This feature is entirely voluntary. You can do so by checking the "Send to the server" checkbox when adding a new word. By default, this option is unchecked.
